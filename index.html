Hello there! Our website revolves around two features: a discussion board and a graph. In the discussion board, anyone can make a post, such as how they are feeling after getting COVID-19, or what symptoms they had. Doctors may also give advice which you can follow. The graph displays the population of people with COVID-19 at the particular location. However, we suggest you start on our about page and read up on our mission statement!
